Optically Powered CMOS Project

- In the project, a fully optical power microchip is designed. It is aimed to operate this microchip even though the light is off. Therefore, the CMOS power management system is created. This project is my Senior Design Project. With this CMOS system created by the contributions of many undergraduate students and professors, mobile phones can be charged wirelessly.
